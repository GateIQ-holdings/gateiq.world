Privacy Policy

Gate IQ, LLC ("Gate IQ," "we," "our") operates gateiq.world. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you visit our site or join our founding list, how we use it, and your choices.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your location with third parties, ever.

Data We Collect

Founding list sign-up — when you reserve a founding spot, we collect:

  • Full name (required)
  • Email address (required)
  • Phone number (required)
  • Home airport IATA code (optional)
  • Traveler type (optional)
  • Whether you opted to save a payment method (optional)

Optional payment method — if you choose to save a payment method at signup, payment details (such as card number, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or PayPal account information) are collected and processed directly by Stripe, Inc. Gate IQ does not receive or store your full payment card number or wallet credentials. Stripe may retain a payment method token, billing details you provide, and limited card metadata (such as card brand and last four digits) on our behalf so we can charge your saved method when Gate IQ launches, if you become a member.

Saving a payment method is entirely optional. You can join the founding list without providing any payment information.

Analytics — if you consent via our cookie banner, we collect anonymized usage data (pages visited, session duration, referral source) through Google Analytics 4. No analytics data is collected without your consent. IP addresses are anonymized.

Automatically collected — standard server logs may capture your IP address, browser type, and request timestamp. These logs are retained for up to 30 days for security purposes only.

How We Use It

  • To notify you when Gate IQ launches and to send your launch box invitation
  • To understand demand by market and traveler type (aggregate, not individual profiling)
  • To improve the product and landing experience based on consent-gated analytics
  • To save an optional payment method for faster checkout when Gate IQ launches, if you choose to provide one
  • To comply with legal obligations

We will not charge you at signup for joining the founding list or for saving an optional payment method. Founding membership billing, if applicable, begins only when Gate IQ launches and you activate or accept membership under the terms in effect at that time.

We will not send marketing emails unrelated to your Gate IQ invitation without your explicit opt-in.

Sharing & Processors

We share data only with processors necessary to operate the service:

  • Formspree — processes founding list form submissions on our behalf. Data is transmitted securely and subject to Formspree's data processing agreement.
  • Stripe — processes optional payment method setup when you choose to save a card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or PayPal for launch. Stripe stores payment credentials and related billing data as our payment processor. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by the Stripe Privacy Policy and Stripe Services Agreement.
  • Netlify — may host serverless functions that securely create Stripe setup sessions on our behalf. These functions receive your signup information only when you initiate optional payment setup.
  • Google Analytics — receives anonymized usage data only if you accept cookies. Governed by Google's data processing terms.
  • GitHub Pages / Cloudflare — hosts the static site. Standard access logs apply.

We do not share your data with airlines, airports, hotel chains, or any commercial partner without your explicit consent at the time of a future product feature.

Data Retention

Founding list data is retained for up to 24 months from the date you sign up, or until you request deletion, whichever comes first. If Gate IQ launches as a product, we will ask for your fresh consent before migrating founding list data into the product.

If you save an optional payment method, Stripe retains that payment information according to Stripe's retention policies and our instructions as merchant of record. You may request removal of a saved payment method by contacting privacy@gateiq.world before launch.

Analytics data (if consented) follows Google Analytics' default 14-month retention period.

Server access logs are deleted after 30 days.

To request deletion of your data at any time, email privacy@gateiq.world with the subject line "Delete my data."

Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Form submissions and payment setup requests are processed over encrypted connections.

Gate IQ does not store full payment card numbers, CVV codes, or wallet credentials on our servers. Optional payment methods are tokenized and stored by Stripe, which is certified to PCI DSS Level 1 standards. We follow industry-standard practices for access control.

If you discover a security vulnerability in our site, please report it responsibly to security@gateiq.world. We will acknowledge reports within 48 hours.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — for analytics cookies, withdraw at any time via our Cookie settings
  • Remove saved payment method — request deletion of a saved payment method before launch by emailing privacy@gateiq.world

To exercise any right, contact privacy@gateiq.world. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EEA and believe we have not addressed your concern, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

Optional Payment at Signup

During prelaunch, you may optionally save a payment method (credit card, debit card, Apple Pay, Google Pay, or PayPal) to speed up checkout when Gate IQ launches. This step is not required to join the founding list.

No charge today — if you choose to save a payment method, you will not be charged at signup. Saving a payment method does not start a subscription or create a billing relationship by itself.
