A note from the founder

Your time is valuable. So is mine.
Wasting time in today's world seems completely backward.

I built Gate IQ because I was tired of watching the best part of travel get eaten by the worst part of it.

I've spent enough time in airports to know the lines, the sprints between concourses, the late-night cancellation leaving one gate agent to hold it all together. And every time, the same thought: all of this is solvable.

So I built the thing I couldn't find: a companion called Glide that watches your entire travel day and quietly takes care of it. Because the vacation is the point. The airport is just in the way — and now it bends to you.

See you at the gate. Or better — past it.
