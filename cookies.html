Cookie Policy

Last updated · May 2026 · Gate IQ, LLC · Nashville, TN

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They allow sites to remember information about your visit — like whether you've accepted this notice — across page loads and sessions.

Gate IQ uses a minimal set of cookies. We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or behavioral profiling.

Cookies We Use

Name Type Purpose Duration
gateiq_cookie_consent Necessary Stores your cookie preference (accept/decline) so we don't ask again. Set in localStorage, not a traditional cookie. Persistent
_ga, _ga_* Analytics Google Analytics 4 — tracks anonymized page visits, session duration, and referral source to help us understand how people find Gate IQ. Only set if you accept cookies. IP addresses are anonymized. 2 years

The necessary localStorage entry is required for the site to remember your consent choice. No analytics cookies are ever set without your explicit acceptance.

Third-Party Cookies

If you accept analytics, Google Analytics 4 sets cookies in your browser. These cookies are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Gate IQ has configured GA4 with IP anonymization enabled and does not enable Google Signals or cross-site tracking.

Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preference at any time using the controls below. You can also clear cookies through your browser settings — most browsers provide options under Privacy or History settings to view and delete stored cookies.

Declining analytics cookies has no effect on your ability to use this site or join the waitlist.
